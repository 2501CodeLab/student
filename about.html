About

The LT Student App is a portal to resources tailored specifically to Lane Tech students. Built to be responsive for smartphones.

You can find various sources of info, such as the school calendar, which will appear compact on your phone. You can also check here to see if there are any special bell schedules for today. We even connect to Lane Tech's database to gather information like the amount of demerits you have, or even see your grades from previous grade levels.

This app is still in development, and we plan to add features like:

  • Checking the amount of service learning hours you have
  • Your current grades, formatted to actually fit your phone unlike CPS' Gradebook
  • Alerts on things like homework assignments, special bell schedules, and any important Lane Tech events.
  • and more!

Be sure to leave some feedback, leave a suggestion on what the app should have, or report any bugs you may come across while using the app!
